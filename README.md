# 2025 Sales Performance Dashboard

## Overview

This project presents an interactive **Sales Performance Dashboard** built in **Power BI** to analyze sales, profit, and margin performance across products, categories, customers, and time. The dashboard is designed for business stakeholders to quickly track KPIs, identify top contributors, and uncover trends that support data-driven decisions.

---

## Objectives

* Provide a consolidated view of **Revenue, Profit, and Margin**
* Identify **top-performing products, categories, and customers**
* Analyze **time-based trends** to understand seasonality
* Enable **quick filtering** for focused analysis

---

## Key KPIs

* **Total Revenue**
* **Total Profit**
* **Profit Margin (%)**
* **Sales by Category & Product**
* **Top Customers by Revenue**

---

## Dashboard Features

* **KPI Cards:** Snapshot of revenue, profit, and margin
* **Category & Product Analysis:** Contribution to overall revenue
* **Customer Analysis:** Identification of high-value customers
* **Trend Analysis:** Monthly/period-wise sales trends
* **Interactive Filters:** Category, product, and time-based slicers

---

## Tools & Technologies

* **Power BI** – Data modeling, DAX measures, and visualizations
* **Excel / CSV (source data)** – Data preparation and validation

---

## Business Insights (Example)

* A significant share of revenue is driven by a small number of categories and customers.
* Certain products consistently outperform others, indicating opportunities for focused sales strategies.
* Time-series trends highlight seasonal spikes that can inform inventory and marketing planning.

---

## How to Use

1. Open the Power BI report (.pbix) or review the exported PDF.
2. Use slicers to filter by category, product, or time period.
3. Review KPI cards for a quick performance snapshot.
4. Drill into visuals to identify trends and top contributors.

---

* Identifying growth opportunities
